02 PRIVACY · BY CONSTRUCTION

Not a promise.
A physical guarantee.

Most "private" AI products send your documents through someone else's servers under a stricter policy. We removed the servers. Your data never touches a network it didn't start on.

A.

On-premise by default

The appliance ships pre-configured. No account creation, no SaaS login, no telemetry phoning home. You own the key, you own the box.

B.

Zero network egress

Inference, embeddings and retrieval all happen on-device. The network interface is optional and can be physically disabled. Air-gap supported.

C.

Compliance-ready

Designed for the frameworks SMBs actually face — GDPR, HIPAA, attorney-client privilege, financial confidentiality. The data footprint never leaves your walls.
